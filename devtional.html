"REFUSE TO ACCEPT THE SATANIC TRAP

📖"And do not lead us into temptation, but deliver us from the evil one."For Yours is the kingdom and the power and the glory forever. Amen."(Mathew6:13NKJV)

Many believers are not really aware that temptation is a reality every believer must encounter.Generally, God does not tempt any man." Let no one say when he is tempted, “I am tempted by God”; for God cannot be tempted by evil, nor does He Himself tempt anyone"( James1:13NKJV).But know this, the Spirit of God can lead us to a place where we face temptation.That is why Jesus instructed His disciples in the Lord's Prayer to ask God not to lead them into temptation.(Mathew 6:13) But the real tempter is Satan and His cohorts. The Bible, speaking about Jesus' temptation, said, "Then Jesus was led up by the Spirit into the wilderness to be tempted by the devil."(Mathew 4:1NKJV). After he had fasted for forty days and forty nights, he was hungry, and here comes the tempter (Satan).Satan said to Him, 'If you are the Son of God, command this stone to become bread." Jesus countered Him with a word." Man shall not live by bread alone,but by every word that proceeds from the mouth of God"( Mathew 4:4). Again, Satan didn't stop here. He took Jesus to a holy city, set him on the pinnacles, and "said to him, if you are the son of God, throw yourself down.For it is written: "He shall give His angels charge over you, and in their hands they shall bear you up, lest you dash your foot against a stone." .Again, Jesus countered him, He said, "It is written, You shall not tempt the Lord your God." (Matthew 4:6-7).
Again, Satan took him to an exceedingly high mountain, showed him all the Glory, and demanded worship from him, but Jesus rebuked him.He said, "Satan, get behind me, for it is written, 'You shall worship the Lord your God.'" And Satan left him(Matthew 4:10).Notice here, first: Satan never gives up in his temptation.He came again when Jesus was on the cross.Second, Satan tempted him using the tools of appetite, fame, and glory.Third, in all these temptations, what Satan wanted was for Jesus to obey and worship him.
You see, as a Christian, know this: Satan may not come physically to tempt you like in the case of Jesus.What he does is to use your appetite for food, sex, or fame to tempt you.He injects negative thoughts into your minds—thoughts of lust, stealing, greediness, jealousy, etc to tempt you.His intent is to make you think his thoughts and do his bidding.Remember, "if you offer yourselves as obedient slaves, you are slaves of the one you obey—either of sin, leading to death, or of obedience, leading to righteousness." (ROM 6:16)
Therefore, don't be ignorant of His trap(II corin 2:11).Refuse to accept Satan's thoughts.Always fill your heart with God's word through meditation, so that you can resist him in the hour of temptation.Think God's thoughts—His Word.Always be ready to counter him with the word.Stand firm and walk perpetually in victory like Jesus.This is the life you have been called to live in Christ Jesus. (I John 5:4)
